About Our Data

There is a lot of information available about COVID-19 and given the huge impact of the virus, we believe it's important that clear and accurate data are available for everyone, regardless of their familiarity with epidemiology, data science or statistics. Everyone's lives have been touched by this virus in one way or another and clear communication and simple presentations of what knowledge we do have is important to give everyone the best possible understanding of the situation we are facing as a country and as a planet.

Data Breakdown:

  • Confirmed cases: the number of positive cases as identified through COVID-19 testing, and also includes presumptive positive cases.
  • Deaths: the number of people who were confirmed positive for the virus and died.
  • Recovered: the number of people who were confirmed positive and have recovered.
  • By state: number of (confirmed, active, etc...) cases per state.
  • Per capita: within a state, the number of per capita "" cases.
