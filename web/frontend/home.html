About COVID-19

COVID-19 is a pandemic that is currently affecting the world in tremendous ways. Especially the US, though it was hit later than most countries, is experiencing enormous strife both as millions of citizens suffer and die from the disease, and the economy continues to decline as stores shut down and companies decrease in revenue.

We want to explore the affect of COVID-19 in the US, but on top of that, we also want to shed some optimistic light these dire times. For example, not many people are aware that a lot more people are getting cured from the disease than there are people dying. Yes the situation seems daunting and scary, and it is, but we don't have to always feel that way. This website is for users to get accurate COVID-19 related data and at the same time have a more positive outlook on the future.
